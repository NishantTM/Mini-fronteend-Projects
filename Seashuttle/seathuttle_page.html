SEA SHUTTLE

The SwiftWave is a fast monohull sea shuttle boutique boat built in 2010, designed for efficient and comfortable passenger transport. It is Commercially Licensed In Greece to carry 12 Passengers. Our vessel is well-equipped with modern navigation, propulsion, and safety systems, making it suitable for reliable coastal and interisland transport and daily trips.
