Welcome to Rhodes! If you're looking for an unforgettable yachting experience, you’ve come to the right place! Whether you choose a half-day or full-day cruise, you'll have the flexibility to explore Lindos and St. Paul’s Bay at your own pace.

*** Why Guests Love Our Cruise: The Top 3 Reasons

  • 1. Flexible Cruise Options: Choose between a half-day or full-day cruise, allowing you to customize your experience based on your schedule and preferences.
  • 2. Explore Iconic Rhodes Destinations: Visit Lindos village and St. Paul’s Bay, soaking in breathtaking views, rich history, and crystal-clear waters. Opt to start from Faliraki to discover hidden gems like Anthony Quinn Bay, Red Sand Bay, Vlicha Beach, and Mavros Kavos along the way.
  • 3. Attention and Personalized Service: Sail aboard ALENA with a professional crew dedicated to providing personalized service, the highest level of comfort and hospitality.

Thank you for joining us. See you on board!
